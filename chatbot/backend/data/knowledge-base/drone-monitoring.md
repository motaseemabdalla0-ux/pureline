# Pure Line SkyHarvest Drone

Pure Line's agricultural drone is called **SkyHarvest**. SkyHarvest is an
autonomous crop-spraying and scouting drone used for precision agriculture.

The SkyHarvest drone has a flight time of 45 minutes on a single battery charge,
and it can cover up to 20 hectares per flight while capturing high-resolution
imagery for crop health analysis.
