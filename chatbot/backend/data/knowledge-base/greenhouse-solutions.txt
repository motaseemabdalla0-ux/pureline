Pure Line Greenhouse Solutions

Pure Line's greenhouse product line is called GreenDome. It is a complete,
automated greenhouse solution built for growers who want reliable, high-value
production throughout the entire year.

GreenDome provides automated climate control, continuously regulating
temperature, humidity, and CO2 levels inside the greenhouse. Sensors monitor the
growing environment and the system automatically adjusts ventilation, heating,
cooling, and CO2 dosing to keep conditions in the ideal range for each crop.

Because the growing environment is kept consistently optimal, GreenDome can
increase greenhouse yield by up to 40% year-round compared to a manually managed
greenhouse. Growers benefit from more predictable harvests and reduced losses
from temperature or humidity swings.
