# Pure Line Smart Irrigation

Pure Line's smart irrigation system is called **AquaSense**. It is designed to
bring precision water management to farms of every size, from smallholdings to
large commercial operations.

AquaSense uses a network of soil-moisture IoT sensors buried in the root zone
together with real-time weather data to schedule drip irrigation automatically.
Instead of watering on a fixed timer, AquaSense only delivers water when the
crop actually needs it, and it pauses irrigation ahead of forecast rainfall.

By matching water delivery to real crop demand, AquaSense reduces water
consumption by 35% on average compared to conventional irrigation, while
maintaining or improving crop yield. The system reports its water savings and
soil-moisture trends through the Pure Line Farm Management Platform.
